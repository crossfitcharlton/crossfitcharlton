CrossFit

CrossFit is a complete, scaleable fitness program designed to meet the needs of beginners as well as experienced athletes. Our program's methodologies include diet, metabolic conditioning, gymnastics, weight training, and sport to ensure everyone can find success and enjoy working out. You'll experience varied, functional movement with high-intensity through classes led by highly qualified trainers. At CrossFit Charlton, our goal is to ensure you have a safe, successful workout and progress you towards your fitness goals (Description courtesy of CrossFit, LLC).

What to expect?

CrossFit is designed to be scalable for individuals of all fitness levels. Our expert trainers will adjust weight, intensity, and mechanics where appropriate to meet your individual needs. During our 60 minute classes, a coach will lead you through warmups then demonstrate the workout with movement instruction. During your workout, the coach will supervise your movements and help you perform them safely and effectively. After your workout you'll feel accomplished, energized, and ready to come back for more!
