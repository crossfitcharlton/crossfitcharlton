About CrossFit Charlton

CrossFit Charlton's mission is to help you, "Make a positive impact on your health through fitness."

What does it take to achieve fitness goals?

  • A brand new, state of the art facility is equipped with the tools to help you succeed.
  • Coaches who are as passionate about health and fitness.
  • A positive community who supports each other on their journey.

We have the skill, equipment, and people to help you achieve your goals at CrossFit Charlton.
