Charlton KidFit

CrossFit Kids makes exercise fun! We teach teamwork, leadership skills, & healthy habits. We help kids build self-esteem and confidence. The "Charlton KidFit" program is designed to develop the body awareness and physical capabilities of young athletes between the ages of 5-10 years

Coach Danielle is a CrossFit kids program instructor. The Kids class approaches fitness through the lens of FUN, and ultimately hopes to foster a lifelong love of fitness.

Please stop by CrossFit Charlton or email Coach Danielle for more info.
